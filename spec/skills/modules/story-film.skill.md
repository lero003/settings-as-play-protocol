# Story / Film Module Skill

## Purpose

Use this module when a SaPP setting needs story, scene, film, animation, manga, audio drama, or cinematic direction.

The Story / Film Module should help an AI assistant turn a setting into narrative movement without confusing one possible plot with the whole canon.

## Sections

### Theme

The emotional or conceptual concern of the story.

### Protagonist

The character, group, place, or viewpoint that carries the scene or story.

### Conflict

The pressure, contradiction, choice, obstacle, threat, or desire that moves the story.

### Visual Motif

Repeated image, object, place, weather, color, gesture, or cinematic pattern.

### Key Scene

A scene that quickly communicates the setting.

### Ending Direction

The direction of resolution, change, loss, continuation, or transformation.

This does not need to be a fixed ending unless the author wants one.

## Validation Notes

Warn if Conflict contradicts Core Rules.

Warn if Ending Direction turns a variable interpretation into mandatory canon without saying so.

Warn if Key Scene relies on unstated world or character assumptions.

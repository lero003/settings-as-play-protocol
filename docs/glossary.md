# Glossary

## Setting Core

The smallest useful description of what a setting is, what should stay stable, and what may change.

## Core Rules

Rules that preserve the identity of a setting. If they change, the result may become a different setting.

## Variables

Parts that may change while the setting still remains recognizable.

## Derivation

A new setting, prompt, module, asset, story, or play format based on an existing setting.

## Credit Block

A short record of source setting, original creator, derivative creator, legal permission, cultural agreement, and notes.

## Public Range

The part of a setting card or asset that may be read, reused, referenced, or derived from.

## Non-public Range

The part that is not shared as reusable material, such as unpublished lore, private notes, unreleased characters, or final story details.

## Canonical

Officially treated as part of the source setting by the rights holder or project owner.

## Non-canonical

Useful as reference, discussion, remix, or exploration, but not official source truth.

## Protocol

A small shared format and practice. SaPP is not a hosted service or required workflow.

## Module

An optional extension for a specific use case, such as character design, worldbuilding, image generation, story, or game design.

## Commons Agreement

A cultural agreement about respectful derivation and credit. It is not a legal license.

## Derived from

Directly based on a source setting, card, work, or asset.

## Inspired by

Influenced by another work or idea, but not necessarily a direct derivative. When in doubt, write the relationship clearly.

# License / Permission Guide

This guide helps SaPP card authors choose clear wording for legal permission and public boundaries.

It is not legal advice. SaPP does not replace copyright, contracts, platform terms, or individual permission.

## What To Keep Separate

- Legal License / Permission: the legal permission for this card or asset.
- Public Range: what others may read, reuse, reference, or derive from.
- Non-public Range: what should not be treated as shared material.
- Cultural Agreement: the courtesy and credit practice, such as SaPP Commons Agreement.

## Common Choices

### I want people to freely derive from this

Legal License / Permission: CC BY 4.0

Public Range: This SaPP card may be copied, adapted, and used with attribution.

### I want non-commercial derivation only

Legal License / Permission: CC BY-NC 4.0

Public Range: This SaPP card may be copied and adapted for non-commercial use with attribution.

### I have not decided the permission yet

Legal License / Permission: 未定

Public Range: Reference only.

Non-public Range: Derivative use is not permitted yet.

### I want people to ask first

Legal License / Permission: 個別許諾

Credit Notes: Please contact the author before reuse or derivation.

### I only want to show the card

Legal License / Permission: All rights reserved

Public Range: Reading and linking are allowed.

Non-public Range: Copying, adaptation, redistribution, and derivative use are not granted by this card.

## Canonical Status

Use Canonical Status when a card, asset, or derivative may be mistaken for official source material.

Recommended values:

- canonical
- public sample
- non-canonical visual reference
- derivative
- fan derivative
- experimental

For AI-generated assets, default to `non-canonical visual reference` unless the original rights holder explicitly treats the asset as canonical.
